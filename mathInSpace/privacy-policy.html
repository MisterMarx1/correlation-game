Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

Mister Marx LLC develops and operates educational games and applications ("the Apps"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information from users of our applications. Our apps are designed to work without requiring users to create accounts, provide personal data, or register any information.

We do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or phone numbers
  • Location data or GPS information
  • Photos, videos, or personal files
  • Social media profiles or contacts
  • Any personally identifiable information (PII)

2. Automatically Collected Data

Our applications may use standard platform services that automatically collect limited technical information for app functionality and improvement:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, hardware model
  • Performance Data: App crashes, loading times, response rates
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous statistics about how features are used
  • Error Reports: Technical information about app malfunctions

This data is collected by platform providers (such as Google Play Services or Apple App Store) and used solely for improving app performance and stability. We do not access or control this data directly.

3. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Our applications are designed for educational use and may be accessed by children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. Our apps are designed to be fully functional without collecting any personal data, making them inherently COPPA compliant.

If you believe a child has provided personal information through our apps, please contact us immediately and we will take appropriate action to remove such information.

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or share any user data with third parties. Our business model does not rely on data collection or sharing.

Any data collected automatically by platform providers is governed by their respective privacy policies:

  • Google Play Services: Google Privacy Policy
  • Apple App Store: Apple Privacy Policy

5. Data Security

While we do not collect personal information, we implement reasonable security measures to protect our applications and any temporary data that may be processed during app operation:

  • Secure coding practices and regular security reviews
  • Use of platform-provided security features
  • Regular updates to address security vulnerabilities
  • No storage of sensitive user data on our servers

Since we do not collect personal information, you have no data to manage with us. However, you have the following rights:

  • Access: You have the right to know what data we collect (which is none)

Our applications may be available internationally. Since we do not collect personal information, there are no international data transfers of user data. Any technical data collected by platform providers is handled according to their own international data transfer policies.
